Jumping Castle Hire in Gaborone: The Ultimate Party Guide (2025)

April 1, 2025 By Avram Kids 5 min read Gaborone, Botswana

If you're planning a kids birthday party in Gaborone, few things will create as much excitement as a jumping castle. Whether it's a pure bounce castle, a thrilling water slide, or a combo unit that does it all — inflatable hire has become the go-to entertainment choice for Botswana families.

But with so many options available, how do you choose the right one? And what should you expect from the jumping castle hire process in Gaborone? This guide covers everything you need to know.

Why Hire a Jumping Castle for Your Party?

Let's start with the basics. A jumping castle is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser for kids of virtually all ages. Here's why Gaborone parents keep coming back to inflatable hire:

  • Hours of entertainment: Kids will bounce, slide and splash for hours without getting bored — giving parents time to actually enjoy the event.
  • Budget-friendly fun: A single inflatable unit entertains dozens of children at once, making it one of the most cost-effective party additions available.
  • No venue required: Most inflatables can be set up in a standard backyard, garden or open lawn — no need to hire an expensive venue.
  • Memorable experience: Children remember inflatable parties for years. It's the kind of thing that gets talked about at school for weeks.

Types of Inflatables Available for Hire in Gaborone

When you search for jumping castle hire in Gaborone, you'll typically come across three main types of inflatables:

1. Pure Jumping Castles

The classic. A bouncing chamber surrounded by inflatable walls and safety netting. These are perfect for pure bounce fun and come in a range of sizes — from compact units for small gardens (3×3m) to large castles (3×7m) that can accommodate many children at once. They typically feature colourful designs with palm trees, arches and cartoon characters.

2. Water Slides

Given Gaborone's warm climate, water slide hire is enormously popular — especially during the hot months. These inflatables feature a raised launch platform, a water-lubricated slide lane and a splash-down pool at the bottom. They're designed for outdoor use and provide a cooling, thrilling experience for older children.

3. Combo Units (Bounce + Slide)

The most popular option in Gaborone by far. Combo units combine a jumping castle with a slide — and often a small water pool at the bottom. You get the best of both worlds: the bouncing fun of a castle plus the excitement of a slide. These are perfect for mixed-age groups and larger parties.

How to Choose the Right Size

Choosing the correct size is critical for both safety and fun. Here are the key factors to consider:

  • Number of children: As a general rule, no more than 6–8 children should bounce simultaneously on most standard units. For larger parties, consider a bigger unit or a combo to spread the kids out.
  • Ages of children: Toddlers (2–5 years) do best on compact, low-height castles with gentle bounce. School-age kids (6–12) can handle larger combos and water slides.
  • Available space: Measure your garden or venue space before booking. Allow at least 1 metre of clearance around the inflatable. Our team can advise on the best fit for your specific space.
  • Indoors vs outdoors: Most inflatables are designed for outdoor use. Water slides must be used outdoors. Some dry castles can work indoors with sufficient ceiling clearance (at least 4–5m).

What to Expect on Booking Day

Once you've chosen your inflatable and confirmed your booking with Avram Kids, here's what to expect:

  1. Confirm your delivery time. Let us know what time you'd like the inflatable set up. We typically recommend 30–60 minutes before the party starts to give us time to inflate and secure everything properly.
  2. Prepare your space. Clear the area of sharp objects, rocks, sticks or garden furniture. The ground should be flat and level. Grass surfaces are ideal — concrete or paving works but requires extra mat protection.
  3. Our team arrives and sets up. The Avram Kids team delivers, unpacks, inflates and securely anchors the unit. This typically takes 20–30 minutes depending on the size of the inflatable.
  4. Enjoy the party! Once set up and safety-checked, we hand over the unit to you. Our team will explain the safety guidelines before leaving.
  5. We collect. After your party, we return to deflate, pack and collect the inflatable. You don't have to do a thing.

Safety Guidelines for Parents

At Avram Kids, safety is our top priority. Here are the key guidelines we encourage all parents to follow:

  • Always have an adult supervising the inflatable at all times.
  • Ensure no shoes, sharp objects, food or drinks are taken inside the inflatable.
  • Don't mix age groups dramatically — keep very young toddlers separate from older, heavier children.
  • Limit the number of children bouncing at one time as advised by our team.
  • For water slides, ensure there's always an adult near the splash pool.
  • In windy conditions, consult with us — very high winds may require the inflatable to be deflated temporarily.
