Parent Peace of Mind

Safety Is Not Optional — It's Our Standard

We know that handing the fun over to us means trusting us with your most precious people. We take that responsibility seriously.

Regular Safety Inspections

Every inflatable undergoes a thorough safety inspection before each hire. We check seams, blower performance, safety netting and anchor points — every single time.

Hygienic Cleaning Protocol

All inflatables are thoroughly cleaned and sanitised after every single hire. Your children will always play on a fresh, hygienic surface — we guarantee it.

Professional Setup & Anchoring

Our team professionally anchors and secures each inflatable at your venue. Nothing is left to chance — we ensure everything is safe before we leave your event.

Age-Appropriate Recommendations

We'll always guide you towards the right inflatable size and type for your children's ages. Toddlers, school-age kids and tweens all have options tailored for them.
